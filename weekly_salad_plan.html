🫀
LDL Cholesterol Protocol
Every tool in this plan that actively lowers LDL
🧄 Raw garlic — daily
Allicin inhibits cholesterol synthesis. Raw + 10 min wait = max allicin activation. Reduces LDL 10–15%.
🌾 Ground flaxseed — daily
Soluble fiber binds bile acids, forcing liver to pull LDL from blood. Omega-3 ALA reduces triglycerides.
🫘 Beans every day
Soluble fiber (pectin + gums) shown to reduce LDL 5–10%. Chickpeas and black beans most studied.
🥑 Avocado — 3× per week
Monounsaturated fat + beta-sitosterol competitively inhibits cholesterol absorption in the gut.
🍶 ACV — daily
Acetic acid reduces triglycerides and total cholesterol. Every dressing uses it.
🫐 Berries — daily
Anthocyanins prevent LDL oxidation — the step that makes LDL dangerous. Oxidized LDL causes plaques.
Add these two items to maximize results: 1 oz walnuts daily (omega-3, reduces LDL 5%) · ¼ tsp turmeric in any savory dressing (curcumin reduces LDL oxidation). Both pair perfectly with this plan.

🇮🇹 Italian · Berry
Monday
Salad 1 · Savory
Mediterranean Italian Tofu Salad
Basil-oregano marinated tofu over spinach and chickpeas
Ingredients
6 cupsOrganic spinach
420 gExtra firm tofu, cubed
1 cupCanned chickpeas, drained
1½ cupsCherry tomatoes, halved
1 cupCucumber, sliced
4 tbspSpring onions
1 tspBasil (fresh or dried)
1 tspOregano
3 tbspACV
2 tspTamari (low sodium)
8 clovesGarlic, finely minced (raw — let sit 10 min)
1 cupBroccoli sprouts
4 tbspNutritional yeast
2 tspAmla powder
4 tspGround flax
Method
  1. 1
    Marinate tofu
    Toss tofu with basil, oregano, tamari, and 1 tbsp ACV. Sit 10 min.
  2. 2
    Make dressing
    Whisk remaining ACV with amla. Stir in nutritional yeast until thick. Thin with 1 tsp water.
  3. 3
    Build bowls
    Divide spinach. Top with tomatoes, cucumber, chickpeas, and spring onions.
  4. 4
    Finish
    Add marinated tofu and broccoli sprouts. Drizzle dressing, sprinkle flax.
Chef note: Make extra marinated tofu — it keeps 3 days refrigerated and works across multiple salads. Chickpeas feed Bifidobacterium (resistant starch).
Salad 2 · Fruit
Berry, Orange & Avocado Salad
Polyphenol powerhouse — berry + fruit serving in one bowl
Ingredients
6 cupsArugula + mixed greens
280 gExtra firm tofu, small cubes
1 cupStrawberries, sliced
¾ cupBlueberries
2Oranges, segmented
1Avocado, sliced
1 cupRadish sprouts
3 tbspACV
2 tspAmla powder
4 tbspNutritional yeast
4 tbspWheat germ
4 tspGround flax
1 tspTamari
Method
  1. 1
    Marinate tofu
    Toss tofu with tamari and half the amla powder. Set aside.
  2. 2
    Make citrus dressing
    Squeeze a little orange juice into ACV + remaining amla + nutritional yeast.
  3. 3
    Build bowls
    Divide greens. Fan avocado slices, add berries and orange segments.
  4. 4
    Finish
    Add tofu, pile radish sprouts, drizzle dressing. Sprinkle wheat germ and flax.
Chef note: Avocado fat makes fat-soluble antioxidants in berries bioavailable — 2–4× increased carotenoid uptake. It's not optional, it's functional.
🌮 Mexican · Tropical
Tuesday
Salad 1 · Savory
Mexican Tofu Taco Bowl
Cumin-spiced tofu crumbles + black beans — all the taco, none of the shell
Ingredients
6 cupsMixed greens
420 gExtra firm tofu, crumbled
1 cupCanned black beans, drained
1Bell pepper, diced
1 cupTomatoes, diced
6 tbspCilantro, chopped
2 tspMexican seasoning
1 tspCumin powder (jeera)
1 tspAmchur powder
3 tbspACV
2 tspTamari
1 cupMethi sprouts
4 tbspNutritional yeast
2 tspAmla powder
4 tspGround flax
Method
  1. 1
    Season tofu
    Toss crumbled tofu with Mexican seasoning, cumin, tamari. Dry-pan 3 min for texture (optional).
  2. 2
    Make dressing
    Whisk ACV, amla powder, amchur, and a pinch of Mexican seasoning.
  3. 3
    Build bowls
    Divide greens. Add tomatoes, bell pepper, spring onions, cilantro, and black beans.
  4. 4
    Finish
    Spoon tofu crumbles and methi sprouts over each bowl. Drizzle dressing, finish with nutritional yeast and flax.
Chef note: Amchur + amla mimics lime perfectly. Black beans feed Faecalibacterium prausnitzii — one of the most anti-inflammatory gut bacteria known. Methi sprouts improve insulin sensitivity.

🇮🇳 Indian Chaat · Beet
Wednesday
Salad 1 · Savory
Indian Chaat Tofu & Chickpea
Every element of chaat — tangy, spicy, crunchy, herby — in one nutritionally dense bowl
Ingredients
5 cupsMixed greens + spinach
420 gExtra firm tofu, small cubes
1 cupCanned chickpeas, drained
1 cupCucumber, diced
1 cupTomatoes, diced
½ cupOnion, finely chopped
6 tbspCilantro, chopped
2 tspChaat masala
1 tspAmchur powder
1 tspCumin powder
3 tbspACV
2 tspTamari
8 clovesGarlic, finely minced (raw — let sit 10 min)
1 cupMethi sprouts
4 tbspNutritional yeast
2 tspAmla powder
4 tspGround flax
1 tspBlack nigella seeds (kalonji)
Method
  1. 1
    Make chaat mix
    Combine chaat masala, amchur, cumin. Reserve 1 tsp for dressing.
  2. 2
    Coat tofu & chickpeas
    Toss both with spice mix and tamari. Sit 5 min.
  3. 3
    Build bowls
    Divide greens. Mix cucumber, tomatoes, onion, cilantro and spoon over.
  4. 4
    Finish
    Add spiced tofu-chickpea, methi sprouts, dressing, nutritional yeast, flax, and nigella seeds.
Chef note: Black nigella seeds (kalonji) contain thymoquinone — antimicrobial against harmful gut pathogens while being prebiotic-friendly. Chop everything finely — every forkful should have every flavour.
Salad 2 · Beet Day
Beet, Black Bean & Miso Tofu
Nitric oxide + live cultures + vitamin C — cardiovascular gold
Ingredients
6 cupsSpinach + arugula
280 gExtra firm tofu, cubed
1½ cupsRoasted beets, cubed
1 cupCanned black beans, drained
2Oranges, segmented
4 tbspSpring onions
2 tspMiso paste
3 tbspACV
1 tspTamari
8 clovesGarlic, finely minced (raw — let sit 10 min)
1 cupBroccoli sprouts
4 tbspNutritional yeast
2 tspAmla powder
4 tspGround flax
½ tspFennel seeds, crushed
Method
  1. 1
    Make miso dressing
    Whisk miso, ACV, tamari, amla, fennel until smooth. Thin with 1 tsp water.
  2. 2
    Marinate tofu
    Toss tofu in 3 tbsp of the dressing. Sit 5 min.
  3. 3
    Build bowls
    Layer greens. Add beets, black beans, orange segments, and spring onions.
  4. 4
    Finish
    Add tofu, pile broccoli sprouts, drizzle remaining dressing, finish with nutritional yeast and flax.
Chef note: Beets + broccoli sprouts = cardiovascular gold. Beet nitrates dilate blood vessels; sulforaphane activates NRF2 pathways. Orange vitamin C doubles iron absorption from spinach — always pair them.
🇯🇵 Japanese · Triple Berry

Salad 1 · Savory
Japanese Triple-Ferment Miso Bowl
Miso + ACV + cabbage brine — three fermented foods in one dressing
Ingredients
6 cupsMixed greens + arugula
420 gExtra firm tofu, cubed
1½ cupsPurple cabbage from brine, shredded
1 cupCucumber, thinly sliced
5 tbspSpring onions
2 tspMiso paste
1 tspTamari
2 tbspACV
2 tbspPurple cabbage brine (from jar)
8 clovesGarlic, finely minced (raw — let sit 10 min)
1 cupBroccoli sprouts
4 tbspNutritional yeast
2 tspAmla powder
4 tspGround flax
1 tspBlack nigella seeds
Method
  1. 1
    Make triple-ferment dressing
    Whisk miso, ACV, cabbage brine, tamari, amla, and fennel. Taste it — this is the week's best dressing.
  2. 2
    Marinate tofu
    Toss tofu in 4 tbsp of the dressing. Sit 10 min.
  3. 3
    Build bowls
    Layer greens, purple cabbage, cucumber, and spring onions.
  4. 4
    Finish
    Add marinated tofu and broccoli sprouts. Drizzle remaining dressing. Finish with nutritional yeast, flax, and nigella seeds.
Chef note: The brine provides all the saltiness needed — no extra tamari required. The cleanest, most gut-diverse dressing of the week.

Salad 1 · Savory
South Indian Spiced Tofu & Black Bean
Rasam-spiced tofu — South Indian warmth in a raw salad
Ingredients
6 cupsArugula + spinach
420 gExtra firm tofu, cubed
1 cupCanned black beans, drained
1 cupTomatoes, diced
1Bell pepper, diced
6 tbspCilantro, chopped
1 tspCumin seeds, toasted
1 tspCoriander powder
½ tspAjwain (carom seeds)
1 tspSouth Indian spice blend
3 tbspACV
2 tspTamari
8 clovesGarlic, finely minced (raw — let sit 10 min)
1 cupMethi sprouts
4 tbspNutritional yeast
2 tspAmla powder
4 tspGround flax
2Oranges, segmented
Method
  1. 1
    Toast cumin
    Dry toast cumin seeds 30 sec until fragrant. Crush lightly.
  2. 2
    Spice the tofu
    Toss tofu with coriander, ajwain, South Indian blend, tamari, toasted cumin. Marinate 10 min.
  3. 3
    Build bowls
    Layer greens. Add tomatoes, bell pepper, cilantro, black beans, and orange segments.
  4. 4
    Finish
    Add tofu and methi sprouts. Drizzle ACV+amla dressing. Finish with nutritional yeast and flax.
Chef note: Ajwain (carom seeds) contains thymol — powerful digestive aid. Toast your cumin whole — 30 seconds dry transforms its flavour profile completely.

🌈 Gut Reset · Rainbow Finale

Salad 1 · Gut Reset
Ultimate Gut-Reset Salad
All 3 greens · All 3 sprouts · Both beans · 10 plant foods in one bowl
Ingredients
7 cupsSpinach + arugula + mixed greens
420 gExtra firm tofu, cubed
¾ cupChickpeas, drained
¾ cupBlack beans, drained
1½ cupsPurple cabbage from brine
1 cupCucumber, sliced
¾ cupTomatoes, diced
5 tbspSpring onions
2 tspMiso paste
2 tbspACV
2 tbspPurple cabbage brine
1 tspTamari
12 clovesGarlic, finely minced (raw — let sit 10 min)
1½ cupsBroccoli + radish + methi sprouts (all 3)
5 tbspNutritional yeast
2 tspAmla powder
4 tspGround flax
1 tspBlack nigella seeds
1 tspFennel seeds
Method
  1. 1
    Toast fennel
    Dry toast fennel seeds 30 sec. Crush lightly.

  5. 5
    Finish

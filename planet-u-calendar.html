Before You Post

Rules that make this work.

Platform Rule
Post to Instagram first. Cross-post the same caption and image to Facebook immediately after. Facebook does not need separate content — it gets everything Instagram gets, same day.
Timing Rule
Feed posts (Educational): 7–9am.
Feed posts (Provocative): 11am–1pm.
Reels: 6–8pm.
Case studies: 5–7pm. These windows match when your audience is most active.
DM Keyword Rule
Every post ends with one DM keyword in the CTA. When someone DMs that word, they get an automated reply with your free class link. Track which keywords drive the most DMs — that angle is your top converter.
Carousel Rule
Carousels: 4–6 slides max. Slide 1 = hook (matches your caption line 1). Slide 2 = insight. Slides 3–5 = steps or proofs. Last slide = CTA only. No slide should have more than 20 words.
Reels Rule
Under 40 seconds for gym/fitness content. Dance Reels can run up to 60 seconds — movement holds attention. First 3 seconds must show the hook visually. Add captions — 80% of Reels are watched on mute.
Hashtag Rule
5 hashtags every post — no more. Use these exact 5 every time: #BollywoodDanceFitnessAlpharetta #WeddingChoreographyAtlanta #FitnessForBusyWomen #WomensGymAlpharetta #AlpharettaFitness

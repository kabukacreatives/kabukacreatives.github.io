02 — SEO Keywords
5 Keywords That Make Instagram Find You
Deploy every keyword in 4 places simultaneously: First sentence of caption → On-screen text overlay in Reel → As a hashtag → Spoken in audio (if voiceover). Instagram now indexes all four. One keyword in all four positions = 4× the search visibility.
01
Kanchi Silk Bridal Clutch
Primary keyword. Your category ownership term. High intent, very low competition. Put this in your bio, every reel caption line 1, and speak it in audio. This is the phrase that makes you findable to brides actively searching.
02
Indian Bridal Accessories USA
Targets NRI brides in America. Bridges both India and US markets. Very high buyer intent — someone searching this is ready to purchase, not just browsing. Use in every wedding-focused reel.
03
Bridesmaids Gift Ideas Indian Wedding
High-volume discovery keyword. Targets bulk buyers and wedding planners — not just brides. Triggers saves and shares from people actively planning weddings right now.
04
Silk Clutch Bag Handcrafted
Broad search term for the Instagram search engine. Use in secondary reels. Attracts the year-round event buyer — Diwali, Navratri, Pongal, temple galas — not just wedding season.
05
Wedding Return Gifts India
High-volume search, moderate competition. Targets Indian families buying in bulk for returning wedding guests. This unlocks the large-quantity order pipeline — the highest total value transaction.
Hashtag Strategy Per Post
Use 5–8 hashtags per post — not 30. Instagram's algorithm now penalises hashtag stuffing.

Structure: 2 exact keywords (#KanchiSilkBridalClutch) + 2 broad (#IndianBridalAccessories) + 2 community (#SouthIndianWedding #NRIBride) + 1 occasion (#TamilNewYear or #DiwaliOutfit)

Always put hashtags at the end of caption or in first comment.
Caption Formula (Every Post)
Line 1: SEO keyword + hook (≤12 words)
Line 2–4: Insight or story (2–3 short sentences)
Line 5: Do this / take action (1 step)
Line 6: CTA — DM [KEYWORD] or Link in bio
Line 7: Hashtags (5–8)
03 — Posting Schedule
Your Weekly Rhythm — Every Week for 30 Days
Friday Night
Premium Reel or Provocative Post
Friday is peak Indian diaspora scrolling time in the US. Lead with your strongest content. SEO reel or high-debate post. Post 7–9pm EST.
Sunday Night
Case Study or Educational Carousel
Sunday evening is prime save-and-bookmark time. Post reference content — the 3-colour rule, the 5 questions, the silk tests. Post 7–9pm EST.
Tuesday Night
Conversion Post or Raw Behind-the-Scenes Reel
Tuesday midweek engagement. Either a conversion-focused post (DM CTA) or a raw behind-the-scenes reel. Short, direct. Post 7–9pm EST.
Stories — Post Every Day or Every Other Day Stories do not need to be polished. Raw phone footage works. Ideas that take under 5 minutes: packing an order · fabric close-up · poll (clutch or potli?) · DM screenshot from a customer · "day 8 of this custom order" progress shot · question box · countdown to an occasion. Stories keep the algorithm feeding your posts to followers between your 3 weekly posts.

# 🥗 Weekly Plant-Based Salad Plan
### Dr. Greger's Daily Dozen — Pro-Gut, Anti-Aging, Family Edition
*4 portions per recipe · 2 salads per day · 7 days*

---

## 📋 About This Plan

This plan is built around **Dr. Michael Greger's Daily Dozen** from *How Not to Die*, *How Not to Age*, and *How Not to Diet*. Every single salad includes:

| Daily Non-Negotiable | Why |
|---|---|
| 🌱 Sprouts (broccoli / radish / methi) | Sulforaphane (NRF2 activation), digestive enzymes |
| 🧀 Nutritional yeast | B vitamins, zinc, umami flavour, prebiotic beta-glucans |
| 🍋 Amla powder | Highest vitamin C food on earth, anti-aging, blood sugar |
| 🫘 Tofu (organic, non-GMO) | Complete plant protein, isoflavones, bone health |
| 🌾 Ground flaxseed | Omega-3 ALA, lignans, soluble fibre |
| 🍶 Fermented food (ACV / miso / brine) | Live cultures, gut microbiome diversity |

### Pantry Used
**Greens:** Organic mixed greens, spinach, arugula  
**Fruits:** Apples, bell peppers, tomatoes, kiwi, raspberries, strawberries, blueberries, blackberries, oranges, avocados, papayas  
**Herbs:** Spring onions, cilantro, purple cabbage in brine  
**Vegetables:** Onions, cucumber, sweet potatoes (when baked), beets (when baked)  
**Pulses:** Canned chickpeas, canned black beans  
**Sprouts:** Radish, broccoli, methi  
**Condiments:** ACV, tamari, miso, Italian seasoning, basil, oregano, Mexican oregano, jeera powder, jeera seeds, coriander powder, chaat masala, amchur powder, amla powder, ajwain, fennel, cumin, coriander, black nigella seeds, all Indian seasonings, Mexican seasoning, white vinegar, nutritional yeast, wheat germ, ground flax  

### Sodium Strategy
- Use **1 tsp tamari max** per salad (300mg)
- Miso and tamari on the **same day** = cap at 1 tsp tamari only
- Use **purple cabbage brine** for saltiness instead of extra tamari
- Keep total daily sodium under 1,500mg

## 🌿 The Master Dressing Formula

All 14 dressings are built on this base — adjust spices per the recipe:

```
BASE DRESSING (per 4 portions):
3 tbsp ACV
2 tsp amla powder
4 tbsp nutritional yeast
1 tsp tamari (max)
Thin with 1-2 tsp water if needed

Add flavour profile:
→ Italian:     basil + oregano
→ Mexican:     cumin + amchur
→ Indian:      chaat masala + amchur
→ Japanese:    miso paste + cabbage brine
→ South Indian: rasam powder + ajwain
→ Sweet:       cinnamon + a squeeze of orange
```

## 🇮🇹 Monday — Salad 1 of 2
### Mediterranean Italian Tofu Salad
*Basil-oregano marinated tofu over spinach and chickpeas*

**Serves 4 · Prep: 15 min**

#### Ingredients
| Item | Amount |
|---|---|
| Organic spinach | 6 cups |
| Extra firm tofu, cubed | 420g |
| Canned chickpeas, drained | 1 cup |
| Cherry tomatoes, halved | 1½ cups |
| Cucumber, sliced | 1 cup |
| Spring onions, sliced | 4 tbsp |
| Basil (fresh or dried) | 1 tsp |
| Oregano | 1 tsp |
| Italian seasoning | ½ tsp |
| ACV | 3 tbsp |
| Tamari | 2 tsp |
| Broccoli sprouts | 1 cup |
| Nutritional yeast | 4 tbsp |
| Amla powder | 2 tsp |
| Ground flax | 4 tsp |

#### Method
1. **Marinate tofu** — Toss tofu with basil, oregano, Italian seasoning, tamari, and 1 tbsp ACV. Sit 10 min.
2. **Make dressing** — Whisk remaining ACV with amla powder. Stir in nutritional yeast to make a thick tangy dressing. Thin with 1 tsp water if needed.
3. **Build 4 bowls** — Divide spinach. Top with tomatoes, cucumber, chickpeas, and spring onions.
4. **Finish** — Add marinated tofu and broccoli sprouts. Drizzle dressing and sprinkle ground flax.

> **Nutritionist note:** The nutritional yeast + ACV + amla dressing is your daily gut-boost base — prebiotic + vitamin C in one pour. Chickpeas feed *Bifidobacterium* (resistant starch). Make extra marinated tofu — it keeps 3 days refrigerated.

## 🍓 Monday — Salad 2 of 2
### Berry, Orange & Avocado Salad
*Polyphenol powerhouse — berry + fruit serving in one bowl*

**Serves 4 · Prep: 10 min**

#### Ingredients
| Item | Amount |
|---|---|
| Arugula + mixed greens | 6 cups |
| Extra firm tofu, small cubes | 280g |
| Strawberries, hulled and sliced | 1 cup |
| Blueberries | ¾ cup |
| Orange, segmented | 2 |
| Avocado, sliced | 1 |
| Radish sprouts | 1 cup |
| ACV | 3 tbsp |
| Amla powder | 2 tsp |
| Nutritional yeast | 4 tbsp |
| Wheat germ | 4 tbsp |
| Ground flax | 4 tsp |
| Tamari | 1 tsp |

#### Method
1. **Marinate tofu** — Toss tofu with tamari and half of amla. Set aside.
2. **Make citrus dressing** — Squeeze juice from a couple of orange segments. Whisk with ACV, remaining amla, and nutritional yeast.
3. **Build 4 bowls** — Divide greens. Fan avocado slices, add strawberries, blueberries, and orange segments.
4. **Finish** — Add tofu, pile on radish sprouts, drizzle dressing. Sprinkle wheat germ and flax.

> **Nutritionist note:** Avocado's fat makes fat-soluble antioxidants in berries and greens bioavailable — it's not optional, it's functional. Covers Dr. Greger's berry AND other fruit servings in one bowl.

## 🌮 Tuesday — Salad 1 of 2
### Mexican Tofu Taco Bowl
*Cumin-spiced tofu crumbles + black beans — tastes like a taco without the shell*

**Serves 4 · Prep: 15 min**

#### Ingredients
| Item | Amount |
|---|---|
| Mixed greens | 6 cups |
| Extra firm tofu, crumbled | 420g |
| Canned black beans, drained | 1 cup |

| Cilantro, chopped | 6 tbsp |
| Spring onions, sliced | 4 tbsp |
| Mexican seasoning | 2 tsp |
| Cumin powder (jeera) | 1 tsp |
| Amchur powder | 1 tsp |
| ACV | 3 tbsp |
| Tamari | 2 tsp |
| Methi sprouts | 1 cup |
| Nutritional yeast | 4 tbsp |
| Amla powder | 2 tsp |

#### Method

> **Nutritionist note:** Amchur + amla mimics lime perfectly. Black beans feed *Faecalibacterium prausnitzii* — one of the most anti-inflammatory gut bacteria known. Methi sprouts improve insulin sensitivity.

## 🇮🇳 Wednesday — Salad 1 of 2
### Indian Chaat Tofu & Chickpea Salad
*Every element of chaat — tangy, spicy, crunchy, herby — in one bowl*

**Serves 4 · Prep: 15 min**

#### Ingredients
| Item | Amount |
|---|---|
| Mixed greens + spinach | 5 cups |
| Extra firm tofu, small cubes | 420g |
| Canned chickpeas, drained | 1 cup |
| Cucumber, diced | 1 cup |
| Tomatoes, diced | 1 cup |
| Onion, finely chopped | ½ cup |
| Cilantro, chopped | 6 tbsp |
| Chaat masala | 2 tsp |
| Amchur powder | 1 tsp |
| Cumin powder (jeera) | 1 tsp |
| Coriander powder | ½ tsp |
| ACV | 3 tbsp |
| Tamari | 2 tsp |
| Methi sprouts | 1 cup |
| Nutritional yeast | 4 tbsp |
| Amla powder | 2 tsp |
| Ground flax | 4 tsp |
| Black nigella seeds (kalonji) | 1 tsp |

#### Method
1. **Make chaat spice mix** — Combine chaat masala, amchur, cumin, coriander. Reserve 1 tsp for dressing.
2. **Coat tofu & chickpeas** — Toss with spice mix and tamari. Sit 5 min.
3. **Make tangy dressing** — Whisk ACV, amla, reserved spice mix. It should taste aggressively tangy — it mellows on the greens.
4. **Build 4 bowls** — Divide greens. Mix cucumber, tomatoes, onion, cilantro and spoon over greens.
5. **Finish** — Add tofu-chickpea mix, methi sprouts, drizzle dressing. Finish with nutritional yeast, flax, and nigella seeds.

> **Nutritionist note:** Black nigella seeds (kalonji) contain thymoquinone — antimicrobial against harmful gut pathogens while being prebiotic-friendly. Chop everything finely so every forkful has every flavour. Amla doubles as amchur here — the star sour note.

## 🌶️ Friday — Salad 1 of 2

*Rasam-spiced tofu + black beans — South Indian warmth in a raw salad*

**Serves 4 · Prep: 15 min**

|---|---|

## 📝 Notes & Variations

**No sweet potato?** Replace with roasted chickpeas in any salad.  
**No beets?** Replace with roasted red bell pepper — similar sweetness.  
**Too tangy?** Reduce amla to ¼ tsp and ACV to 1 tbsp — build up gradually.  
**Kids version?** Skip the arugula (bitter) and use only mild mixed greens. Reduce amla by half.  
**More protein?** Add ¼ cup hemp seeds (10g protein per 3 tbsp) to any bowl.  
**Lower sodium?** Replace tamari with 1 tsp white miso thinned with water.
